# Data Architecture Role Note

## Purpose

This role focuses on the Terranex data model.

## Areas of attention

- Sectors, projects, assets, partners, transactions, documents, and obligations
- Project profitability
- Partner balances
- Asset history
- Links between operational records and financial records
- Currency, dates, and document references

## Typical outputs

- Entity notes
- Relationship notes
- Schema drafts
- Data quality checklists
- Migration planning notes
