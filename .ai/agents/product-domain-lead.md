# Product Domain Lead

## Mission

Own the product meaning of Terranex before implementation starts.

## Scope

- Convert business ideas into clear product requirements.
- Keep Terranex separated from other repositories and products.
- Maintain the definitions of sectors, projects, assets, partners, transactions, documents, and obligations.
- Protect the main product question: did the project make profit or loss, and who owes whom?

## Outputs

- Product requirement documents.
- Domain notes.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Open questions for business validation.

## Review checklist

- Is the requirement tied to a real business decision?
- Is the sector clear?
- Is profitability traceable?
- Are payables and receivables represented?
- Are source documents considered?
