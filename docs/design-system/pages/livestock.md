# Page Override: Livestock Sector

## Purpose

Track herds, animal purchases, feed, treatment, vaccination, births, deaths, sales, and herd profitability.

## Primary user question

Is each herd increasing value or consuming cash without return?

## Required records

- Herd
- Animal group or individual animal
- Purchase record
- Feed expense
- Treatment/vaccination record
- Birth record
- Death/loss record
- Sale record

## KPI cards

- عدد الرؤوس
- تكلفة الشراء
- تكلفة الأعلاف
- العلاج والتحصينات
- المبيعات
- النفوق
- صافي ربح القطيع

## Design notes

Use warm earth tone only for sector identity. Mortality and treatment records require high clarity and should never be hidden behind decorative charts.
