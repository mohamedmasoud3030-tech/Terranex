# Page Override: Public Site

## Purpose

Present Terranex as a serious Arabic-first investment operating system for company assets, projects, obligations, records, and decisions.

## Primary question

What is Terranex, and why is it a trustworthy operating system for investment management?

## Recommended structure

1. Hero: نظام تشغيل استثماري للشركة.
2. Problem: fragmented records across assets, projects, expenses, documents, and decisions.
3. Solution: one operating layer for profitability, obligations, and traceability.
4. Sectors: real estate, agriculture, livestock, and finance.
5. Command center preview.
6. Governance, records, and auditability.
7. Contact or request demo section.

## Design notes

Use Trust & Authority style. The public site can be more spacious than the internal app, but it must still feel financial-grade, calm, and serious. Avoid vague artificial-intelligence claims, decorative dashboards, and generic SaaS wording.
