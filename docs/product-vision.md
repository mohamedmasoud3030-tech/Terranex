# Terranex Product Vision

Terranex is a standalone investment operating system for managing company assets and projects across three investment sectors.

## Core sectors

1. Real estate investment: land, assets, acquisition, development, sale, cost, and profit.
2. Agricultural investment: farms, crops, seasons, production, expenses, sales, and season profitability.
3. Livestock investment: herds, purchases, feed, treatment, vaccination, births, mortality, sales, and herd profitability.

## Main business question

The product exists to answer:

- Did this project make profit or loss?
- Who owes money?
- Who should be paid?
- Which documents support the answer?

## Product principle

Terranex should be built around traceable business truth, not disconnected screens. Every financial answer should be linked to source transactions, contracts, invoices, payments, and operational records.
