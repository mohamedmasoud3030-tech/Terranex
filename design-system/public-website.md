# Public Website
## Layout rules
- Trust-first corporate layout: hero, sectors, governance, contact.
## Components needed
- Hero panel, sector cards, stats strip, timeline, contact form.
## KPI cards
- Assets under management, active projects, partner network, years operating.
## Tables
- Limited use: disclosures or governance table.
## Filters
- Not required except media/news archive.
## Forms
- Contact/investor inquiry form.
## Badges
- Real estate, agriculture, livestock, governance.
## Charts
- Optional high-level allocation chart.
## Empty states
- For news/media section only.
## Accessibility checklist
- Keyboard nav, semantic headings, form validation.
## RTL rules
- Full RTL including navigation and hero CTAs.
## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Startup-style playful gradients and crowded animations.
