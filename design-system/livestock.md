# Livestock
## Layout rules
- Herd overview KPIs + health and finance records.
## Components needed
- KPI cards, herd table, treatment log, sales log.
## KPI cards
- Total herds, current count, care cost, herd profitability.
## Tables
- Herd, species, headcount, feed cost, treatment cost, sales, net.
## Filters
- Species, herd status, health flag, date range.
## Forms
- Add herd, record treatment, register mortality, sale entry.
## Badges
- Healthy, monitored, critical, sold.
## Charts
- Herd growth and cost trend.
## Empty states
- "No herds recorded."
## Accessibility checklist
- Error text for numeric inputs, keyboard-safe log tables.
## RTL rules
- Metric units consistently rendered.
## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Missing audit trail for care costs.
