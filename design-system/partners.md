# Partners
## Layout rules
- Counterparty portfolio + obligations focus.
## Components needed
- KPI cards, partner table, outstanding balances panel.
## KPI cards
- Total partners, receivables exposure, payables exposure, disputed balances.
## Tables
- Partner, role, projects, receivable, payable, net position, status.
## Filters
- Role, balance type, risk status, sector.
## Forms
- Add partner, define role, balance adjustment with justification.
## Badges
- Investor, supplier, buyer, debtor, creditor.
## Charts
- Top 10 counterparties by exposure.
## Empty states
- "No partners defined."
## Accessibility checklist
- Role tags text-readable, form validation.
## RTL rules
- Financial totals aligned consistently.
## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Mixing partner role semantics.
