# Real Estate
## Layout rules
- Asset portfolio cards above a detailed properties table.
## Components needed
- KPI strip, asset map placeholder, properties table, valuation panel.
## KPI cards
- Total properties, current valuation, sale-ready assets, occupancy.
## Tables
- Asset ID, location, type, acquisition cost, valuation, status.
## Filters
- Region, asset type, status, valuation band.
## Forms
- Add property, valuation update, legal document attach.
## Badges
- Owned, developing, listed, sold.
## Charts
- Valuation distribution by region.
## Empty states
- "No real estate assets registered."
## Accessibility checklist
- Map substitute list, field labels, contrast checks.
## RTL rules
- Location labels in Arabic with controlled LTR for coordinates.
## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Image-first listings without financial metrics.
